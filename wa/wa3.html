History

The Los Angeles Lakers were established in 1948 as the Minneapolis Lakers. Their name the "Lakers" reflects Minnesota's nickname "Land of 10,000 lakes." The team moved to Los Angeles in 1960 and has since become one the most successful franchises in the NBA because who cares about the Celtics. The Lakers have won 16 championships in their history and have had many Hall of Fame players such as Magic Johnson, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, and Kobe Bryant. They are the first and only NBA franchise to win a Mickey Mouse NBA championship in Disney World

Arena

The Los Angeles Lakers play their home games at the Staples Center, which is located in downtown Los Angeles. The arena is also used by Los Angeles Clippers, LA Kings, and for various concerts. On this website I will not use the arenas new name because it is dumb.
